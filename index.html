Nuclear Engineering • Physics • Astrophysics | UW–Madison

I am a Nuclear Engineering student at the University of Wisconsin–Madison with research interests in nuclear security, nonproliferation, and nuclear war effects.

My work focuses on computational modeling of radiation transport, shielding, and high-altitude nuclear environments using Python and MCNP.

I have conducted research at Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory and Oak Ridge National Laboratory and plan to pursue a Ph.D. in nuclear security.
